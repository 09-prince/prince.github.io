Prince Gour

Prince Gour is an AI & Generative AI Engineer who completed his Bachelor's in Computer Applications (BCA) with a specialization in AI & Cybersecurity from Poornima University, Jaipur (2022–2025).

He previously worked as an AI Engineer at NeuroNex Labs, where he focused on fine-tuning domain-specific Large Language Models using LoRA/PEFT techniques and optimizing end-to-end inference pipelines for efficient deployment.

Currently, he is dedicated to AI research and development, with interests in Large Language Models, Transformer architectures, and Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback (RLHF). He is passionate about building AI systems that bridge language understanding with real-world problem-solving.
